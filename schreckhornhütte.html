SCHRECKHORNHÜTTE SAC, BERNESE OBERLAND

05.07.2021

Difficulty: Difficult T4 - Ascent 1380m - Descent 1380m

Distance: 15km

Duration: 6h30

Route:

Train to Grindelwald

Cable car to Pfingstegg

Hike: Pfingstegg - Schreckhornhütte SAC - Pfingstegg

Cable car to Grindelwald

Icefall between Obers and Unders Ischmeer

The path from Berhaus Bäregg to the Schreckhornhütte SAC is an alpine route that leads along a flock of sheep,
landslides and a spectacular view on the crevasses in the icefall.

Kleines Fiescherhorn/Ochs - 3895m, Fieschgrat - 3740m and spectacular crevasses
in the upper part of the icefall between Obers and Unders Ischmeer

View from Schreckhornhütte SAC - 2530m across Obers Ischmeer, far left Finsteraarhorn - 4274m

View from Berghaus Bäregg - 1775m on the Grindelwald glacier. We could observe several small ice avalanches.
